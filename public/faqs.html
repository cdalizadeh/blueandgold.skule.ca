FAQs

Q: What is this "Blue and Gold Committee?"

You can check out the home page of this site, but here's the tldr: The Blue and Gold Committee is a UofT Engineering spirit committee that organizes community events, manages tools and design resources available to students, and bridges the gap between what you learn in class and what you build in the real world. If you ever need to borrow tools, or need some help with a practical design project, or have an awesome idea for a fun event we can host, LET US KNOW. We're down to talk about whatever you have in mind.

Q: How do I borrow tools?

Feel free to use the Tools Sign-out Form, and we'll get back to you with a confirmation of time and date! Or just email us. Or message us on Facebook. We're not picky.

Q: What can I do to get involved?

Join our Mailing List. Keep an eye out for our first event, the Pride Parade!

Q: Can my club borrow tools, too?

For the most part, yes. We're happy to loan tools to other clubs on a short-term basis. Email us at blueandgold@skule.ca and we can discuss the logistics of it all.
